1. Linear Regression

S
Tabular
Fits a straight-line relationship between input features and a continuous numeric target.
Use case: Predicting a house's sale price from square footage, location, and age.

2. Logistic Regression

S
Tabular
Estimates the probability of a binary outcome using a sigmoid over a linear combination of features.
Use case: Predicting whether a credit-card transaction is fraudulent.

3. Decision Tree

S
Tabular
Splits data on the most informative feature at each node, building a tree of yes/no questions.
Use case: Sorting fruit by color, size, and weight (1.4 assignment).

4. Random Forest

S
Tabular
Trains many decision trees on bootstrapped data samples and averages their votes for stability and accuracy.
Use case: Predicting customer churn from CRM history.

5. K-Means Clustering

U
TabularCV
Groups data into K clusters by iteratively assigning points to the nearest centroid and re-computing centers.
Use case: Customer segmentation for a retailer's marketing campaigns.

6. K-Nearest Neighbors

S
TabularCV
Classifies a new point by majority vote of its K closest neighbors in feature space.
Use case: Recommending similar products via user-item proximity.

7. Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)

S
Computer Vision
Stacks convolution and pooling layers to learn local-to-global spatial features from images.
Use case: Detecting tumors in medical scans; reading street signs for autonomous driving.

8. Recurrent Neural Networks / LSTM

S
NLPTime Series
Processes sequences step-by-step while carrying internal state, capturing temporal dependencies.
Use case: Forecasting stock prices; speech-to-text transcription.

9. Transformer

S
NLPGenAICV
Uses self-attention to weigh relationships across all tokens in a sequence in parallel — powering modern LLMs.
Use case: Powers ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and most modern AI assistants.

10. Generative Adversarial Network (GAN)

U
GenAICV
Two networks (generator vs discriminator) train against each other; the generator learns to produce realistic samples.
Use case: Generating photorealistic faces, art, and synthetic training data.
